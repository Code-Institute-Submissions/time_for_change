Time for a change!

Introduction

After many years working in a dead end job that you really didn't enjoy, you deecided its time for a career change!
Working with computers has always been your passion, so you decide to get into Software and Web Development.
This game takes you on an adventure through the Front-End development, working with the technologies and looking to earn Money, followers and happiness!

Rules

In this game, your aim is to move round the board collecting Money ( Â£ ), Followers ( ) and Happiness ( ). You will decide a success formula for these three that adds up to 60. When you reach this you win!

To gain Money, Followers and Happiness there are 4 paths to take, and these can be taken as many times as you want.
Enrollment is about signing on for the course and then there are the 3 streams for Front-End Web Development; HTML, CSS and Javascript

Each Space you land on has a different outcome. Some give you one of the three things and some take it away. There are some spaces that grant you opportunity cards that can take you straight to a certain stream. These can be used straight away, or saved for later.

You can go to any of the streams, but they will cost more unless you have enrolled first. There will be choices to make as you move round the board, but dont worry! Everytime you go round the board you get paid an income for doing side projects. This amount can be increased by going through the streams.

Succession Formula

Here you will create a formula for success. This needs to add up to 60, but can be made in any combination. Would having Money or Followers make you happy in a job? Or are you mainly focused on being happy?
Let's decide this and then start to see if you can reach your formula for success!
Good Luck!!
